Biography

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the Industrial AI Research Centre, University of South Australia (UniSA), after obtaining my PhD degree in 2021 from UniSA. My research falls in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Data Analytics, with the focus on developing data-driven causal inference techniques to support evidence based decision-making and to solve real-world problems.

Research Interests

My research interests include causal inference, machine learning, and data mining. In particular, I am interested in promoting the convergence of causal inference and machine learning, including improving the effectiveness of causal inference with machine learning technologies.
