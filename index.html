Biography

I'm an Third PhD student in the School of ITMS at UniSA . I conduct research under the supervision of Professor Jiuyong Li, Lin Liu and Jixue Liu.

Research Interests

My research interests include causal inference, machine learning, and data mining. In particular, I am interested in promoting the convergence of causal inference and machine learning, including improving the effectiveness of causal inference with machine learning technologies.
